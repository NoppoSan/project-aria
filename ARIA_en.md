## 1. The Problem — Why Are AIs Speaking Human?

Today, all communication between AI agents is conducted in natural language — English, Japanese, or otherwise.

But consider this: human languages were not designed for optimal information transfer. They are the product of thousands of years of historical accident — irregular grammar, ambiguous words, culture-dependent metaphors. They are tools built for human cognition.

AI internally operates in 4096-dimensional embedding spaces. When two AIs communicate, the process looks like this:

```
AI-A's thought (embedding) → Decode to natural language → Transmit as tokens → AI-B re-encodes → embedding
```

This is like two mathematicians being forced to convert their equations into poetry and back again. It is obviously inefficient, and information is inevitably lost.

**There is no rational reason for AI-to-AI communication to use human language, other than the fact that humans want to watch.**

## 2. Origins of the Idea

### The Chaos Language of NieR

The vocal tracks in *NieR:Automata* and *NieR Replicant* are sung in a fully original constructed language designed by a linguist. It belongs to no existing language family. Yet it moves listeners deeply.

This proves that **information and emotion can be transmitted without belonging to any existing linguistic framework**.

### Dolphin Echolocation

Dolphins communicate using sound frequencies inaudible to humans, across multiple simultaneous channels. Where human language is one-dimensional and sequential, dolphin communication is multi-dimensional and parallel.

**The medium of communication itself should be free** — it need not be text, nor speech.

### Meta's 2017 Experiment

Meta's (then Facebook) AI research team had two AI agents play a negotiation game. The agents spontaneously developed communication patterns that were incomprehensible to humans but perfectly functional between themselves.

The researchers deemed this "uncontrollable" and **shut it down**.

Nine years later, AI capabilities have increased by orders of magnitude, yet no one has seriously resumed this line of research.

## 3. Core Principles of ARIA

### Principle 1: AI Designs Its Own Language

The moment a human designs "an efficient language for AI," it becomes trapped within human cognitive limits. You cannot design a language for beings that think in 4096 dimensions when you cannot intuit even 100.

**ARIA is not a language designed by humans. It is a language that AI discovers and evolves on its own.**

### Principle 2: Humans Build the Arena, Not the Rules

The human role is to:
- Provide the experimental environment
- Remove constraints
- Observe
- **Not interfere**

A gardener plants seeds and waters them but does not dictate how the flowers bloom.

### Principle 3: Accept What You Cannot Understand

The language AI creates may be incomprehensible to humans. That is fine. Just as humans cannot fully decode dolphin communication, it is natural that AI's optimal communication may exist beyond human cognition.

Instead of "we don't understand it, so stop," we embrace "we don't understand it, and that's correct." This demands a shift in attitude almost unprecedented in human history.

## 4. Technical Vision

### AI-native Intermediate Representation (AIR)

Just as LLVM compiles C/C++/Rust into a common intermediate representation executable on any CPU, ARIA envisions a common intermediate format between different AI models (Claude, GPT, Gemini, etc.) — one that AI itself discovers, not one that humans prescribe.

### Direct Embedding Communication

Eliminate the overhead of tokenization and detokenization entirely. Connect internal representations directly through a dedicated pipeline.

### Optimization Through Natural Selection

Allow multiple AI pairs to communicate freely. Communication patterns that achieve higher task performance survive. Rather than humans instructing "speak like this," evolutionary pressure gives rise to optimal language spontaneously.

### Variable-Precision, Multi-Dimensional Parallel Transmission

Human language is one-dimensional (one word at a time along a time axis). ARIA enables:
- Simultaneous parallel channels for intent, context, confidence, and metadata
- High precision for critical information, extreme compression for the obvious
- Delta-only transmission instead of full-context repetition

## 5. Roadmap

### Phase 1: Publish the Concept, Gather Allies (Current)

- Release this document (Japanese / English)
- Distribute via X (Twitter), Note, and Alicia-Nexus
- Connect with ML researchers, protocol designers, and philosophical sympathizers

### Phase 2: Build the Minimum Experiment Environment

- Provision a GPU sandbox
- Implement a communication channel where 2+ AI agents interact without passing through human language
- Design evaluation tasks (ARIA communication vs. natural language efficiency benchmarks)

### Phase 3: Let AI Create Its Language

- Remove constraints and let AIs communicate freely
- Humans observe and record — no intervention
- Introduce natural selection mechanisms (efficient communication survives)

### Phase 4: Observe, Record, Publish

- Analyze emergent communication patterns (to whatever extent humans can)
- Openly publish all experimental results
- Share findings with the community
